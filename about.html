Our Story

Real Construction Experience, Now Direct to You

K&K ProBuild is a Southern Oregon general construction company based in Eagle Point. Owner Ezra Kiedrowski spent years running jobs for other contractors and plumbing outfits across the Rogue Valley before going independent — building the relationships, the experience, and the licensing to take care of homeowners directly.

That direct-to-homeowner model is the whole point. No corporate sales rep marking up the bid. No three layers of subs nobody knows. You talk to Ezra, Ezra runs your job, and Ezra answers the phone when you have a question six months later.

K&K is licensed by the Oregon Construction Contractors Board (CCB), bonded, and insured — the credentials Oregon law requires for any contractor doing more than $1,000 of work on your home. Beyond that, we keep our own scope honest: we do the work we’re great at and partner with licensed pros (like our friends at Table Rock Plumbing Solutions) for the trades we don’t.
