02 / HVAC

Mini-Split Heating & Cooling

Over 100 mini-splits installed across Southern Oregon. Mini-splits are how we cool the upstairs bedroom that an ancient HVAC system never quite reaches, heat the converted garage that’s now an office, and keep the whole house comfortable on a budget that beats a full HVAC retrofit.

  • Single-zone installs (one room or addition)
  • Multi-zone systems (whole house, multiple heads)
  • Mounting, electrical, and condensate routing
  • Top-tier brands: Mitsubishi, Daikin, Pioneer, and more
  • Sized correctly for your space — no oversold tonnage

As an Oregon-licensed general contractor, K&K ProBuild is permitted to install ductless mini-split systems in residential applications.
